# About This Site

## Who Prepared This

Rasmus "Cervator" Praestholm -- West Orange resident, parent of three children
in the district, PTA member, and volunteer with a local youth sports league.
By profession, a software engineer.

## Disclaimer

These proposals were assembled aspirationally on behalf of the community. While
the author is affiliated with local organizations including a PTA and a youth
sports league, **no organizations have been committed to any of the ideas
presented here**, and no promises are made about specific outcomes or savings.

Not every idea in these documents will be viable. Some may be false leads, and
some may duplicate efforts the district has already undertaken. Because the
district's processes are largely not public, we have no way to know what has
already been tried or considered.

The intent is to:

- **Start a conversation** about what the community can do to help
- **Demonstrate capacity** -- one person produced this in a few days; imagine
  what a coordinated community effort could achieve
- **Offer a framework** for community-district collaboration, not a finished plan

## How This Was Made

These documents were researched, drafted, and organized over a few days using
modern AI-assisted writing tools, web research, and publicly available data
(news coverage, NJ state reports, legislative records, and published audits).
All specific claims are sourced with links to primary or secondary sources --
see [References & Source Links](20-references) for the full bibliography.

The site is built with [Jekyll](https://jekyllrb.com/) and the
[just-the-docs](https://just-the-docs.com/) theme, hosted on
[GitHub Pages](https://pages.github.com/). The source is public at
[github.com/SiliconSaga/schools](https://github.com/SiliconSaga/schools).
